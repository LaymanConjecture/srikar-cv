Overview

A founder who ships AI systems that compose cryptography, multi-agent reasoning, and posttraining into a single deployable stack.

The thread across my work is the same: take a decision that matters — underwriting a loan, scoring an anonymous borrower's creditworthiness, choosing what a market should believe — and rebuild it as a system where every step is auditable, every belief is calibrated, and every outcome retrains the model. My current focus is custom Axion deployments inside large capital allocators — firms whose decisions are too consequential to leave to general-purpose models.

Applied AI
Posttraining stacks for high-stakes decisions.
GRPO and PPO via VeRL, hybrid retrieval, LLM-as-judge evals, hard-example mining, and scaling from 8B to 70B — targeted at the full decision (which question to ask, which evidence to weight, when to defer, when to act), not just calibration. Built and deployed inside customer security boundaries on proprietary signal that no closed API can ever see.
Cryptography
Provable systems where trust is the product.
PhD at NYU Courant on cryptography + ML. Co-author of one of the first provably-fair credit-scoring systems shipped to production (Spectral), and early advisor to Scroll. Comfortable across zkML, MPC, TEEs, and identity-bound key delegation.
Multi-Agent Systems
Agent topologies that reason in parallel and disagree visibly.
Eternis builds agent stacks where many objects — borrowers, assets, questions, evidence, outcomes — each have scope, memory, and a reviewable trail. Disagreement is treated as signal, not noise; every decision is decomposed before a human acts.
Operating principle: a small team scales by scaling the use of agents. The same principle Eternis deploys for customers is the one used internally. Three people with a clean posttraining loop can outwork an org of hundreds operating on closed-source APIs.

Selected Work

The contributions I would defend in front of a research panel.

Each card is a system I designed, built, or led — chosen because it produced a research-grade artifact, a production deployment, or both.

Verifiable ML · Spectral
First provably-fair on-chain credit-scoring system.
Made under-collateralized DeFi lending cryptographically auditable for the first time. Borrowers can prove their credit decision was computed correctly without revealing private data or model weights. A provable-decision-tree primitive years ahead of the zkML research wave.
Posttraining · Eternis
In-firm posttraining stack for capital allocators.
Full pipeline — SFT, DPO, RLVR with verifiable outcomes — targeted at the full decision rather than calibration alone. Customer-private data never leaves the security boundary. Inference economics rebuilt on open-source models at 10–100x lower cost than closed APIs.
Multi-Agent · Axion
Agent topology for credit and forecasting decisions.
Many parallel agents reasoning over assets, borrowers, evidence, and questions; disagreement is surfaced as signal before a human decides. Every action has a scope, a memory, and a reviewable trail. Live posteriors and underwritten priors live alongside market views in a single belief graph.
Prediction Markets · EIV
Prediction-market platform from primitive to product.
Designed and built a markets platform at venture studio EIV. The same primitive now powers serious AI-evaluation infrastructure (Polymarket, Metaculus FutureEval). Forecasting evals at Eternis trace directly to this work.
